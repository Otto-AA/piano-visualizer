Welcome

This website is designed, to provide a simple and free visualization tool for piano recordings. All you need are a MP3 and a MIDI file.

Features

  • MIDI Visualization
  • Audio Visualization
  • Online Storage (up to 40MB)

Getting Started

(When you are having an error at any step, just contact me and I will help :)

Account

  1. Signup and verify your email address.
  2. Login
    You should now have a folder at http://player.bplaced.net/user//data/ where all your files are stored.
    Your visualizations will be visible at http://player.bplaced.net/user//

Upload

  1. Open this page: http://player.bplaced.net/user//manage/ and click on Upload new Song
  2. Fill out the form (required are a name, a mp3 and midi file)
  3. Click submit and wait while uploading.
  4. Your song will be visible at http://player.bplaced.net/user//
  5. If you want to change your song afterwards, you can do that here: http://player.bplaced.net/user//manage/

Record video
There is no built-in recorder but you can do as follows

  1. Install a screen recorder (I use the free program IceCream)
  2. Go to your visualizations site and choose the song you want to play.
  3. Enter the fullscreen mode (F11 in most browsers)
  4. You can use following shortcuts:
    Space -> Play/Pause
    T -> Toggles the menu
  5. Start the song and record :)

About

This website is created by A_A, a newbie to webdesign and coding. Originally it started as a little piano, where my website visitors can play a little themselves. Then I added the possibility to let the piano play my songs, so people can see which keys I pressed (and maybe can learn it better themselves). Later on I gave it a visual rework and also added the audio visualization. And now I've simplified the upload process and opened it for others to use aswell. Lets see what will come in the future ;)

Everything here is powered by bplaced.net, a free webhost. Therefore this website is free to use, but with several limits (maximum number of users and storage)
